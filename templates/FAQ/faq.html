What are CNC Machines? ‚ñº
CNC stands for Computer Numerical Control. CNC machines are automated systems that use computer programming to control the movement and operation of tools and machinery. They are commonly used in manufacturing to perform tasks such as cutting, drilling, milling, and shaping various materials with high precision and efficiency.

The prediction engine on the CNC Machine Prediction website utilizes machine learning algorithms and historical machine data to forecast the performance of CNC machines. This helps operators and managers make informed decisions to maximize machine uptime and productivity.
